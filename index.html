Magic

Magicicinas are known to be able to cause supernatural phenomenas, be it in the world of fiction or the real world. In the real world there would be no real magic. They are just tricks that they use to perform their show. Although they are hard to understand from time to time. In the world of fiction, there where the real magic exist, is completely different from the magic in our world. Their magic is like mathematics or physics in our world . They need to understand the concept of it and the formula in order to even use the basics of magic. For them to get the magic to manifest they need a special energy source, that energy source is called mana. Everyone is born with mana according to their world some differ from each other like the the amount of mana or the quality. The amount of mana is called the mana pool, some people are born with large amount of it and some may have so little mana that it's unnoticeble.

Adventures

In the world of fiction most of the time it will be played in the Middle Ages where swords and knights exist. Except there is a significant difference. There are monsters there. Monsters that could endanger humanity. but of course there are people that fight the monsters. those are adventures. they explore the unexplored and fight the unbeatable and make a impossible possible. And it makes good money. of course there are mages who uses magic to fight the monsters. In the late Middle Ages there will be academies for the children of nobles to attend so that they can learn more about magic. Although mostly noble children attend it there might be exceptions for commoner children.
